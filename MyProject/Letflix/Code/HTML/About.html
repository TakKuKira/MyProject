History
Letflix is a application designed by FarelJokoProductionHouse since 1998. It all started with the idea to rent DVDs by mail to the creation of the first DVD rental and sales website. We offer unlimited DVD rentals without limits and advanced our website in 2000 to introduce a personalized movie recommendation system. Then, in 2002 LeTflix decided to make its initial public offering at $1 a share. As membership grew globally, we also further improve our services by launching our product accross all devices Now at Letflix, you can find any movies, TV shows, and others anywhere. We're provide more than 20 languages from different country. In a single subscription you can control what u want to watch, no ads, and you also can download a movies or TV shows so you can watch in offline.
